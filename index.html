About Me

I am a computer engineering student at the University of California, San Diego, studying within the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department. I’m from South Gate, CA, a predominantly Latinx community, and I attended a high school where 99% of the students share this background. I have a year of experience in game development, and my career goal is to become a game designer or programmer, with the long-term dream of founding my own game studio to create immersive 4X strategy games inspired by companies like Paradox Interactive. As a Latinx transgender woman, I am deeply committed to increasing transgender visibility in the gaming industry. This purpose drives my ambition to develop inclusive games and foster spaces where everyone can see themselves represented.

My Work

Crown & Conquest

Crown & Conquest is a dynamic 4X strategy game developed with Unreal Engine 5.5. Players select a ruler and aim to acquire crowns from neighboring kingdoms to achieve victory. The gameplay involves exploring the fog of war to map uncharted territories, expanding the empire by crowning neighboring kingdoms, exploiting newly acquired territories for resources, and exterminating hostile kingdoms to bring them under control. With its immersive mechanics and strategic depth, Crown & Conquest delivers an engaging experience that blends exploration, conquest, and resource management.

Marble Escape

Marble Escape is a captivating 3D puzzle game built with Unreal Engine 5.5. In this thrilling challenge, you have 5 seconds to manipulate the puzzle in the current direction by pressing the Spacebar. The direction changes every 5 seconds, eventually cycling back to the original direction. Strategically time your moves to guide the marble in the right direction to escape.

Zombie Shootout

Zombie Shootout is a 2D top-down shooter developed with Unreal Engine 5.5 where you play as Tim, a truck stop worker stranded on a desolate highway. Armed with his trusty AK-47 and a determination to survive, Tim must defend a critical bridge that connects his highway to another. As hordes of zombies close in, it’s up to Tim to hold the line and prevent the undead from overrunning the area until help arrives.

The Last Petals of Spring

The Last Petals of Spring is an engaging Yuri visual novel developed with Unreal Engine 5.5. Centered around Aya, a young protagonist navigating heartfelt choices among her close friends, the story unfolds primarily in Aya's room as players guide her through dates and deepening connections. Each playthrough offers a unique experience, revealing emotions and strengthening bonds, with the freedom to explore all three romantic paths upon completing a route.
