About Me

I’m a computer engineering student at the University of California, San Diego, studying in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department. I’m from South Gate, CA, a predominantly Latinx community, and attended a high school where 99% of students are also Latinx. My career goal is to become a software engineer or game programmer, with a long-term dream of founding my own game studio to create immersive 4X strategy games inspired by companies like Paradox Interactive. As a Latinx transgender woman, I am deeply committed to increasing transgender visibility in the gaming industry. This purpose fuels my ambition to develop inclusive games and spaces where everyone can see themselves represented.

My Work

The Last Petals of Spring

The Last Petals of Spring is an engaging Yuri visual novel developed with Unreal Engine 5.5. Centered around Aya, a young protagonist navigating heartfelt choices among her close friends, the story unfolds primarily in Aya's room as players guide her through dates and deepening connections. Each playthrough offers a unique experience, revealing emotions and strengthening bonds, with the freedom to explore all three romantic paths upon completing a route.
